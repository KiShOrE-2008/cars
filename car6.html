Mahindra Scorpio N

About the Car

The Mahindra Scorpio N is a rugged, powerful SUV built for adventure and comfort. With its bold new design and modern tech features, it's a strong contender in the full-size SUV segment in India.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 2.0L mStallion Turbo Petrol / 2.2L mHawk Diesel
  • Horsepower: Up to 200 hp
  • Drivetrain: 2WD / 4WD options
  • Transmission: 6-speed Manual / Automatic
  • Price: ₹13.6 – ₹24.5 Lakh (approx.)

Why Buy?

The Scorpio N combines off-road ability with urban luxury. It’s an ideal SUV for Indian roads, families, and long-distance adventures, packed with safety and smart tech features.
