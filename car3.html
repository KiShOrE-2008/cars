Ferrari 488

About the Car

The Ferrari 488 is a high-performance sports car that combines speed, design, and technology. It features a 3.9L twin-turbocharged V8 engine and is built for exhilarating drives and pure Italian craftsmanship.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 3.9L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 670 hp
  • Top Speed: 330 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.0 seconds
  • Price: ₹3.9 Crore (approx.)

Why Buy?

The Ferrari 488 is made for those who value performance, elegance, and legacy. It's not just a car — it's an experience on wheels, known for precision handling and iconic sound.
