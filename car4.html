Tesla Model S

About the Car

The Tesla Model S is a groundbreaking all-electric luxury sedan that redefined performance and sustainability. With instant torque and a sleek minimalist design, it’s a favorite among modern car lovers.

Key Specifications

  • Motor: Dual Electric Motors (AWD)
  • Horsepower: Up to 1020 hp (Plaid Model)
  • Top Speed: 322 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 2.1 seconds
  • Range: Up to 600 km per charge
  • Price: ₹1.7 Crore (approx.)

Why Buy?

Model S combines cutting-edge technology, silent power, and zero emissions with luxury and safety. It's a smart choice for those who want speed with a conscience.
