BMW M5

About the Car

The BMW M5 is a high-performance luxury sedan that blends power, precision, and everyday comfort. Known for its aggressive design and thrilling driving experience, it’s a favorite among performance enthusiasts.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 617 hp (M5 Competition)
  • Top Speed: 305 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.3 seconds
  • Price: ₹1.7 Crore (approx.)

Why Buy?

The BMW M5 offers thrilling acceleration, precision handling, and the luxurious practicality of a four-door sedan. It’s the ultimate sleeper supercar for everyday use.
